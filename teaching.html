Teaching

Dr. Ding teaches graduate courses in survival analysis and applied statistical modeling in the Department of Biostatistics. She was awarded the University of Pittsburgh's James L. Craig Excellence in Education Award in 2021.

Mentoring & advising

Beyond the classroom, Dr. Ding advises doctoral and master's students in biostatistics and supervises graduate student researchers. Her advisees have earned ENAR Distinguished Student Paper Awards, ICSA and Lifetime Data Science student paper awards, and departmental teaching and research honors, and have gone on to positions in academia and industry.

Current and former lab members are listed on the People page. Prospective students are welcome to reach out.
